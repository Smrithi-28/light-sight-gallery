Light & Sight Gallery

Discover the fascinating science behind light, vision and optical illusions.

Welcome

Light is an integral part of our lives. In earlier times, it was believed that light consisted of rays travelling in straight lines from the eye to the object, and that sight occurred when these rays touched the object. Through centuries of scientific research, we now have a much deeper understanding of what light is and how it behaves.

About the Gallery

The Light & Sight Gallery showcases the properties and applications of light, along with the mechanisms that enable human vision. Through interactive exhibits, working models and multimedia presentations, visitors can explore fascinating concepts related to light and perception.

Understanding Vision

This hall helps visitors understand how we see, why two eyes are important, common defects of the eye, and the role of the self-controlling iris.

Interactive Exhibits & Illusions

Visitors can explore optical illusion exhibits, Bending of Light in Pipes, Captive Shadow, Addition of Colours, Optical Fibre Lighting, Anamorphosis and many other fascinating demonstrations showing that seeing is not always believing.
